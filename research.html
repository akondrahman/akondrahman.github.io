My Research Interests


In my undergraduate research I worked to find an energy-efficient scheme to track multiple targets in wireless sensor network. My research effort was successful and resulted in publications.


My current research is focused on Continuous Deployment, DevOps, and Agile Software Engineering. In Fall, 2014 I co-reviewed papers submitted for International Conference of Software Engineering, 2015.

I am currently investigating continuous deployment practices by mining internet resources. So far I have studied the practices of 19 companies including Facebook, Github,and Netflix. I have reported my recent findings here.

Investigating security practices of DevOps shops

Oct 09, 2015

The adoption of DevOps in different IT organizations is increasing day by day. However, many software professionals are concerned about the security of software in such organizations. In my current research project I am investigating the perception of software security in DevOps shops.

My Research Interests


My current research is focused on Continuous Deployment, DevOps, and Mining Software Repositories. In Fall, 2014 I co-reviewed papers submitted for International Conference of Software Engineering, 2015.

I am currently investigating the perception and adoption of software security in DevOps organizations. Stay tuned for my latest findings!
